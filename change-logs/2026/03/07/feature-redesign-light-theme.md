Redesign light theme to premium quality. Status colors now use jewel tones (deep teal, indigo, emerald, etc.) instead of neon colors that looked washed-out on white backgrounds. Background gradient is more saturated, glass effects have stronger blur and contrast, shadows use blue-tinted layers for depth, and column glow is more prominent. Added `useStatusColors()` hook and `useResolvedTheme()` hook for theme-aware color resolution across all components.
